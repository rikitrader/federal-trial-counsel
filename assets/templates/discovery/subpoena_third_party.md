## RULE 45 REQUIREMENTS

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Issuing Court** | Court where action is pending |
| **Service** | Personal service required |
| **Notice to Parties** | Before serving on non-party |
| **Geographic Limits** | Within 100 miles of where person resides/works |
| **Time for Compliance** | At least 14 days for document production |
| **Costs** | Must tender fees for 1-day attendance + mileage |
