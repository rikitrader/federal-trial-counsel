## STRATEGIC USE OF ADMISSIONS

### Purpose Categories:

| Category | Strategic Purpose |
|----------|------------------|
| **Authentication** | Authenticate documents to avoid hearsay objections |
| **Undisputed Facts** | Establish facts for summary judgment |
| **Element Satisfaction** | Lock in elements of claims/defenses |
| **Cost Shifting** | Under Rule 37(c)(2), recover costs if denied matters later proved |
| **Impeachment** | Use denials for impeachment at trial |

### Effect of Admission:

- **Conclusively established** for purposes of pending action
- Cannot be contradicted at trial
- Withdrawal requires court permission under Rule 36(b)

### Effect of Failure to Respond:

- Matter is **DEEMED ADMITTED**
- No motion to compel required
- Automatic after 30 days
