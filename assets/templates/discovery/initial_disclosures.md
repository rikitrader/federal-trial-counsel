## RULE 26(a)(1) CHECKLIST

### Required Disclosures:

- [ ] **Witnesses**: Name, address, phone of individuals likely to have discoverable information + brief description of subjects
- [ ] **Documents**: Description and location of documents, ESI, and tangible things in your possession that you may use
- [ ] **Damages**: Computation of damages claimed + documents supporting calculation
- [ ] **Insurance**: Copy of any applicable insurance agreement

### Timing:

| Event | Deadline |
|-------|----------|
| Rule 26(f) Conference | Per Court Order |
| Initial Disclosures | **14 days after** Rule 26(f) Conference |
| Supplementation | As information becomes known (Rule 26(e)) |

### Exceptions (No Disclosure Required):

- Actions exempted by Rule 26(a)(1)(B):
  - Review of administrative record
  - Forfeiture actions
  - Petition for habeas corpus
  - Pro se prisoner litigation
  - Actions to enforce arbitration awards
  - Actions to collect student loans

### Supplementation Duty (Rule 26(e)):

Must supplement if:
- Information is incomplete or incorrect
- Additional information becomes known
- Unless the additional information has already been made known through discovery or in writing
