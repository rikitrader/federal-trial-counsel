# ELEVENTH CIRCUIT APPEAL WORKFLOW

## Overview

This workflow guides the appellate process from entry of judgment through oral argument in the U.S. Court of Appeals for the Eleventh Circuit.

---

## STANDARDS OF REVIEW REFERENCE

| Issue Type | Standard | Burden on Appellant |
|------------|----------|---------------------|
| Legal questions | De novo | Must show legal error |
| Mixed law/fact | De novo for legal standard; clear error for facts | Show error in either |
| Factual findings | Clear error | Must be clearly erroneous |
| Discretionary rulings | Abuse of discretion | Must show no reasonable basis |
| Procedural rulings | Abuse of discretion | Must show prejudicial abuse |
| Unpreserved issues | Plain error | Must show (1) error, (2) plain, (3) affecting substantial rights, (4) seriously affecting fairness |

## KEY 11TH CIRCUIT DEADLINES

| Action | Deadline | Rule |
|--------|----------|------|
| Notice of Appeal | 30 days (60 if govt party) | FRAP 4(a) |
| Transcript order | 14 days after notice | FRAP 10(b) |
| Civil Appeal Statement | 14 days after notice | 11th Cir. R. 33-1 |
| Appellant's brief | 40 days after record | 11th Cir. R. 31-1 |
| Appellee's brief | 30 days after appellant's | 11th Cir. R. 31-1 |
| Reply brief | 21 days after appellee's | 11th Cir. R. 31-1 |
| Rehearing petition | 14 days after judgment | FRAP 40 |
| Certiorari petition | 90 days after judgment | S. Ct. R. 13 |
